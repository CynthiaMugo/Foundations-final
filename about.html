Fiction Addicts Bookclub. Welcome!

About Us

Welcome to the wild, whimsical world of Fiction Addicts — where stories come alive and readers never leave empty-handed (or empty-hearted)! We're a passionate community of bookworms who live for gripping plots, unforgettable characters, and late-night chapter binges. Whether you're into magical realms, tear-jerking dramas, mind-bending mysteries, or laugh-out-loud rom-coms, you’ll find your tribe here. So grab a cup of tea (or coffee—we don’t judge), crack open a book, and join us on this literary adventure.

Meet the Founders

Fiction Addicts was brought to life in 2022 by three passionate book lovers who couldn't resist the pull of a good story and a warm cup of coffee. Meet Peter Maina, Ariel Omondi, and Zamani Wekezo—the dynamic trio who started this community to bring fiction lovers together and spark endless conversations. Click to learn more about them

Together, they’ve created Fiction Addicts as a space where readers can escape reality, dive into books, and connect over their shared love for mystery, romance, and beyond. They are always open to suggestions from members, love planning exciting community meetups, and most importantly, they never say no to a good book discussion over a hot cup of coffee.
